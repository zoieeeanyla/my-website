Legal Environment of Business

The legal environment of business involves the set of laws, regulations, and ethical standards that guide businesses in their operations. Understanding these laws helps businesses operate within legal boundaries, avoid risks, and make informed decisions. The laws related to business may vary depending on the jurisdiction, but they cover aspects such as contracts, consumer protection, environmental regulations, intellectual property, and more.
