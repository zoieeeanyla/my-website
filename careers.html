Careers in the Legal Environment

The legal environment offers exciting and impactful career opportunities. Whether you're interested in starting as a paralegal, becoming a corporate lawyer, or pursuing forensic accounting, the possibilities are endless. Below are some key roles, their requirements, and how they contribute to the field.

Entry-Level Career: Legal Assistant

Legal Assistants are essential to any law office, providing administrative support, managing documentation, and organizing case files.

  • Skills Needed: Attention to detail, organization, basic legal knowledge.
  • Requirements: High school diploma or certification program.
  • Salary Range: $40,000 - $50,000 annually.
